Gap Year

I took a year off between high school and college to live abroad through the Rotary Youth Exchange program. Rotary was originally a business/social club oriented around public service (I think they championed the search for a polio vaccine). Now, they pay for high schoolers to live in a different household somewhere around the world for a year.
