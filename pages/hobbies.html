Running

I like to run. My interest was sparked while I was abroad in Chile after high school. I played soccer and lacrosse--poorly, but very competitively--throughout high school and really most of my life. I needed some physical outlet, but living on a farm did not lend itself very well to team sports. Even Chilean rodeo is an individual endeavor if you don't count the livestock. Long story short, I started running a lot. I did not have any goal in mind other than to generally exhert myself, something I have at times regretted when I wish I was faster than I am.

While my leg speed might have suffered from aimless miles, I did develop two things that have since served me quite well: an pretty dank aerobic base; and more importantly, the ability to clear my mind and find solace while running.
